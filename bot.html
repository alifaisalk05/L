What is This Bot?

This Telegram bot helps users find the best Amazon deals every day. It converts regular product links into affiliate links, suggests trending items, and even sends exclusive coupon-based products. It’s optimized for mobile use and lightning fast. Users can interact via chat commands or inline buttons.

Why It’s Useful

With thousands of products and price drops, it’s hard to keep up. This bot does all the heavy lifting—scanning, selecting, and linking deals for users. It’s a perfect tool for online shoppers, gift finders, and deal hunters, and it brings value right into Telegram.

How It Works

Users simply paste an Amazon link or type a keyword, and the bot returns a clickable affiliate link or matching product suggestions. These links include my Amazon Associate ID and redirect to the original Amazon product page, enabling tracking and commission eligibility.
